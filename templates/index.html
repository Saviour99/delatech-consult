Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, development process, and pricing.

What types of websites do you develop?
We develop a wide range of websites including personal blogs, business websites, e-commerce platforms, portfolios, and custom web applications tailored to your specific needs.
What is your development process?
Our process includes consultation, planning, design mockups, development, testing, and launch. We maintain clear communication throughout and provide regular updates on progress.
How long does it take to develop a website?
The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website can take a few weeks, while a more complex web application might take several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.
How much does it cost to develop a website?
Our pricing varies based on the project's complexity, features, and timeline. We offer competitive rates and provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements. Contact us for a personalized estimate.
Do you offer website maintenance and support?
Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to keep your website updated, secure, and performing optimally. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, and technical support.
